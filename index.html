Difficulties Level

There are 3 different levels :

  • Beginner
  • Intermediate
  • Advanced

The higher the level, the more words will be in play and the harder it will be to guess.

How to play?

You have 6 tries to guess the word.

Only valid words are allowed. Hit enter to submit your guess.

With each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show you how close your guess is to the word.

  • S is in the word and in the correct place.
  • N is in the word but in the wrong place.
  • P and O are not in the word.

How scores are calculated?

  • +10 for every correct letter, in the correct place.
  • +5 for correct letters, in the wrong place.
  • -3 for letters not in the word.
