How to play?

You have 6 tries to guess the word.

Only valid words are allowed. Hit enter to submit your guess.

With each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show you how close your guess is to the word.

  • S is in the word and in the correct place.
  • N is in the word but in the wrong place.
  • P and O are not in the word.
